Extinction the Game....
You are the head of an entire species. You are responsible for migration and reproduction. You must compete and prey with other species.
The board is divided into numerous hexagons representing different habitat areas. Six different gene cards are drawn to dictate the different aspects of life. Players take turns drawing environmental change cards that affect them and sometimes all players. The game continues until only one species survives.
This game involves strategy and can take sudden unexpected twists. Build your populations strength but be careful if you get too overcrowded, You may fall victum to diesease and end up EXTINCT.
